Our pricing
Our main focus is '4.99' service. It means AWWCOR Inc provides a full cycle and ongoing support for contract recruitment for $4.99 per hour. The $4.99 fee is calculated as follows: if a desired hourly rate of the offshore full-time contractor is not higher than $50 per hour then our fee is $4.99 per hour. Our services are not limited to listed models, you can always get more information on special cases by Contacting us

Discount proposal
Group hiring

The discount applies to the full-cycle recruitment in relation to group hiring for positions of the intern or junior level. In that case, AWWCOR fee is calculated as follows: if you hire 5 or more specialists (intern or junior level) per request on long-term WFH basis with identical job spec (not less than 5000 hours for the group) then our fee is $2.99 per hour.

Long-Term Relationship Discount

The discount is applied to the full-cycle recruitment of huge groups of specialists on a long-term WFH basis. In that case, AWWCOR fees are calculated as follows: if the client maintains a total number of full-time associates of 50 or above contractors:
- Every associate who worked over 1-year has AWWCOR fee decreased from $4.99 to $4.49 (10%);
- Every associate who worked over 2-years has AWWCOR fee decreased from $4.49 to $3.99 (20%);
- Every associate who worked over 3-years has the rate decreased from $3.99 to $3.49 (30%).

No further rate decreases after 4 years ($3.49 rate continues); If the total headcount drops below 50 then AWWCOR fees is changed to the original fees in the next billing cycle. Replacement of any existing associate will constitute a regular (not discounted) fee. The discount is applied to associates, hired with the original fee of $4.99 per hour.

Getting Out of Contract

AWWCOR does not offer ‘Contact to Hire’. Any conversions from contract to FTE basis for any existing or new customers are offered by legitimate MSA termination as follows: the customer should pay a one-time fee of $9999 per associate and compensation fee of $999 for each month from the date the job scpec provided, including all legal fees if needed.

You can stop cooperating easily and safety in case you don’t need contact services any longer, without payment of any compensation fees. Such termination of the Contract takes place if you are not going to have any direct or indirect relationship with existing AWWCOR associates, as well as their partners, clients, and any other parties involved and also in case you agree on our Policy.
